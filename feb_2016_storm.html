The Great Storm of February 2016!

Note:This website/page is not associated with Monroe County is any way.

Hello.

My name is Tim Duffy, and I maintain MCSafetyFeed.org. It grabs a feed of non-violent 911 calls from the Monroe County Dispatch center every minute or so, and keeps them in a database. You can see today's incidents here. I made the site about 3 years ago, and it's been collecting ( some times a bit unreliably :/ ) incident data since then.

We had quite the storm on February 16th, 2016 up in Monroe County. I thought I would put together a small data dump of the data that I was able to capture.

First, some background: I don't get all 911 calls. I only get a collection of them. I do get a unique ID with each call, though, so I can get an approximate total number of calls each day.

The dispatch center get's aver 3 MILLION calls each year - that's over 10,000 a day on average!

  • On February 16th, 2016 the center got ~3700 calls.
  • On February 16th, 2015 the center got ~3200 calls.
  • On February 1st, 2014 the center got ~3000 calls ( Feb 2014 the data from the county was spotty :/ ).
  • On February 16th, 2013 the center got ~3000 calls.
